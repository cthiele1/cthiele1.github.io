Problem statement: Food for College Students

Healthy and nutritious food is not widely available to college students. A lot of college students are stuck eating "junk food" which negatively impacts their diets.

Affinity diagram:Food for College Students

This diagram does a great job of listing the major ideas associated with college students getting better access to healthy and nutritious food. The five main sections all the ideas fall under include "long term", "shipping/transportation", "advertising/marketing", "costs', and "dietary options". These five groupings are important because they help us map out all the ideas that are associated with our problem. It is easier to see the ideas all mapped out at once, rather than a couple at a time.
